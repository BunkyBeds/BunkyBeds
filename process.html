How does the whole process work?

  1. Accurately measure the width, length, and height of the room where the bunk bed will be placed.
  2. Measure the width, length, and height of your mattress. This will help determine the appropriate size for the bunk bed, or please specify if you require a single or 3/4 size bed.
  3. Select two colors for your custom bunk bed.
  4. Send all the above details to me via email, along with your city of residence, or feel free to contact me via WhatsApp.
